Ebonyi State University (EBSU) School Fees 2022/2023

The article provides information on the Ebonyi State University (EBSU) School Fees for the 2022/2023 academic session, catering to both new and returning students. The school management has published the tuition fees online, offering a detailed breakdown for the benefit of both existing and fresh students.

The content includes a breakdown of school fees for fresh indigenes and non-indigenes. For new indigenous students, the registration fee is NGN 12,000, school fees are NGN 88,000, resulting in a total of NGN 100,000. On the other hand, new non-indigenous students have a registration fee of NGN 22,000, school fees of NGN 128,000, totaling NGN 150,000.

The article encourages readers to check the outlined details for a comprehensive understanding of the fees payable at Ebonyi State University. The information aims to guide both new and returning students in preparing for the academic session.

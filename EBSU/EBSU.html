2023/2024 UTME (Jamb) Admission Requirements for Ebonyi State University (EBSU)

In this post, you will have more insight into EBSU general admission requirements for 2022/2023 before applying to study an undergraduate course or program this year.

EBSU Admission Requirements For 2022/2023

Below are the requirements for candidates that wish to gain admission into EBSU through JAMB (UTME):

  • A minimum of 5 credits in the Senior Secondary Certificate Examinations (SSC/GCE) or NECO, for those using NECO results.
  • Choose the correct JAMB subject combination for your course.
  • Score at least a minimum of 160 and above in JAMB to stand a chance of gaining admission into EBSU.
  • Make EBSU your First Choice of Institution.
  • Participate in EBSU POST UTME/SCREENING exercise.
  • Candidates must have reached the age of 16 and above.

EBSU Requirements For Direct Entry (DE) 2022/2023

For direct entry candidates, here are the requirements:

  • A minimum of 5 credits in the Senior Secondary Certificate Examinations (SSCE/GCE) or NECO. Candidates must also meet up with such Faculty/Departmental entry requirements.
  • Degree (First Class/Second Class Upper) in related fields.
  • Direct entry candidates are required to purchase the EBSU Direct Entry form through JAMB and EBSU Portal.
  • A minimum of Merit Pass in the National Certificate of Education (NCE), National Diploma (ND), and other Advanced Level Certificates.

DEPARTMENT OF BIOLOGY

Courses that train students to appreciate life and the structure of living things are mounted in the Department of Biological Sciences of Federal University Lokoja; as it is obtainable in any other conventional University. Biology, Botany, and Zoology are adequately taught, each as a body of knowledge and/or application of the knowledge for enhancement of man’s economy and survival. The Department has qualified and experienced lecturers employed for the job delivery. Our students are given broad-based training during the first two years in the Department. Students in good standing, by the third year, specialize into the three degree awarding programmes of B.Sc Biology, B.Sc Botany, and B.Sc Zoology. The undergraduate programmes are undertaken through coursework, seminars, field trips, semester examinations, research, and any other modes as may be prescribed by the National University Commission.

DEPARTMENT OF INDUSTRIAL CHEMISTRY

Industrial Chemistry is the index of industrial development everywhere in the world. The enormous strides made by man in the understanding, exploitation of nature and synthesis of new products all have their roots in chemistry and chemical technology. For economic sustenance and technological breakthrough, the Industrial Chemistry undergraduate programme is designed to encompass an appreciation of the centrality of chemical sciences in the entire undergraduate curricula.

The Department of Industrial Chemistry is one of the newly established (2019) departments in the Federal University, Lokoja, under the Faculty of Science, offering a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Chemistry.

DEPARTMENT OF MICROBIOLOGY

The Department of Microbiology at the Federal University Lokoja, established in 2019, operates in alignment with the university's vision and values. Currently offering undergraduate programs in Microbiology with future plans for Postgraduate programs, the department focuses on a comprehensive educational approach, including coursework, practical training, seminars, and research projects. Led by dedicated academic and non-academic staff, the department aims to equip students with knowledge and skills contributing to societal improvement. Courses emphasize the complexities of life, environmental interrelationships, and practical applications for economic activities and survival. The faculty comprises qualified lecturers committed to effective knowledge transfer through various teaching methods..

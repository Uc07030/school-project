Direct Entry Undergraduate Admission Requirements

Minimum Requirements for Direct Entry Admissions

Candidates for Direct Entry can gain admission through various qualifications, including IJMB/GCE A’Level, NCE, OND, HND, and Bachelor’s Degree, each with a corresponding minimum aggregate points ranging from 0 to 16.

Programme Overview:

Economics
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit passes, including English, Maths, Economics, and any 2 relevant Arts or Social Science subjects.
  • Subject Requirements: Economics, Mathematics, and 1 other relevant subject.
  • Additional Qualifications: 2 A’ Level Passes in Economics and one of Maths, Statistics, Geography, Financial Accounting, Business Management, History, or Government. Also, a Merit pass in 2 NCE teaching subjects, including Economics.
English
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit passes, including English, Literature in English, and any 3 of either Arts or Social Science subjects.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes, including Literature and one other Arts or Social Science subject. Alternatively, a Credit Pass in 3 Subject areas, including Literature in English.
Geography
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit passes, including English Language, Mathematics, Geography, and any two from Arts and Social Sciences or Science.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes in Geography and any one of History/Government, Economics, or Biology/Agricultural Science.
History
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 SSC credit passes, including History/Government, English Language, and any other three from Arts or Social Sciences.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes in History/Government and any other Arts or Social Science subject.
Political Science
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Maths, Government/History, plus two Social Science Subjects and any other subject.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes to include History/Government and one other Arts or Social Science.
Biology
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes to include Biology/Botany/Zoology and Chemistry, Maths, or Physics.
Chemistry
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes in Chemistry and any other of Mathematics, Physics, or Biology.
Computer Science
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Mathematics, Physics, and any other two Science Subjects.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes in Maths and any one of Physics, Chemistry, or Biology/Zoology.
Geology
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and any other relevant science subject.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes from Geology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics.
Mathematics
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Maths, Physics, and any two of Chemistry, Further Maths, Geography, or Biology/Agriculture.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes to include Mathematics and any one of Physics, Geography, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, or Statistics.
Physics
  • O’Level Requirements: 5 Credit Passes, including English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and any one of Biology, Geography, or Agricultural Science.
  • Subject Requirements: 2 A’ Level Passes to include Physics and any one of Mathematics and Chemistry.

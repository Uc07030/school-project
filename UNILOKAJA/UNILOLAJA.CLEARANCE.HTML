2022/2023 ADMISSIONS EXERCISE – IMPORTANT NOTICE TO ALL APPLICANTS

The University has commenced admissions upload on the 2022/2023 CAPS platform.

All 2022/2023 candidates who chose Federal University Lokoja as their first choice are advised to visit the nearest JAMB Office or JAMB-Accredited CBT Centre to access their e-Facility and check their admission status.

PART A: Admission Processing

Admission processing shall be in two (2) categories:

  1. Candidates admitted into the Programme of their choice should proceed to accept or reject the admission offered.
  2. Candidates transferred to programmes other than their choice are expected to accept or reject the transfer offered by the institution. If a candidate accepts the offer of transfer, he or she should subsequently accept the admission offered in the new Programme.

Kindly note:

  • Admission upload shall be in batches; visit your JAMB e-facility regularly to check your admission status.
  • Once you receive an offer, respond within 48 hours, otherwise, you will forfeit the offer.
  • Ensure that your O’level result(s) are uploaded on your JAMB e-facility to avoid automatic disqualification.
  • Uploading of O’level result(s) and acceptance of transfer/admission offer(s) are best carried out at a JAMB-Accredited CBT Centre or JAMB Office.
PART B: GUIDELINES FOR THE 2022/2023 FRESH STUDENTS ONLINE REGISTRATION (UTME & DE)

The University portal is opened for the 2022/2023 ONLINE Registration Exercise.

Eligibility conditions for clearance:

  1. Your admission must have been approved on the Central Admission Processing System (CAPS) of JAMB.
  2. You must have accepted the offer of admission as approved by JAMB.

Read the guidelines below before commencing the process:

GUIDELINES FOR THE REGISTRATION EXERCISE
  1. STEP 1: ACCEPTANCE OF OFFER
    1. Visit the University website (www.fulokoja.edu.ng) and click on Admissions – Undergraduate – Post-UTME. Login with your JAMB Registration Number to confirm your admission status.
    2. Once successful, click on Pay Now to process the payment for acceptance charge of N20,000.00 using REMITA services (card or bank payment).
    3. Return to the University website and click on Admissions – Undergraduate – Post-UTME Portal to confirm your payment.
    4. You will be provided a school email address, which would be the official means of communication.
    5. Print your Admission Letter and Letter of Undertaking.
  2. STEP 2: O’LEVEL RESULT VERIFICATION
    1. Return to the University website and click on Admissions – Undergraduate – Post-UTME Portal – Submit O’level to supply your O’level examination and scratch card details. Note that failure to upload the correct O’level examination details and a valid examination scratch card will lead to automatic disqualification of admission. If you had previously submitted your O’level Scratch Card details, kindly proceed to No.2 below.
    2. Check your university email or the Post-UTME Portal for confirmation to proceed with your registration. Once successful, proceed to step 3.
  3. STEP 3: PAYMENT OF SCHOOL CHARGES
    1. Visit the University website and click on Student – Undergraduate – Student Portal. Login with your JAMB Registration Number.
    2. Click on Pay Now to process the payment for School Charges using REMITA services (card or bank payment).
    3. Return to the University website and click on Student – Undergraduate – Student Portal to confirm your payment.
    4. After confirmation of payment, print out your School Charges receipt.
    5. Proceed to print out the completed online STUDENT BIO-DATA FORM (Form 01).
  4. NOTE: You are advised to keep all printed documents as you will be required to submit them when you resume.
  5. NOTE: STEP 4 & 5 WOULD BE CARRIED OUT ON RESUMPTION
    1. STEP 4: MEDICAL CERTIFICATE AND COURSE REGISTRATION
      1. MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF FITNESS: Visit the University Health Services Centre to conduct your medical fitness test.
      2. COURSE REGISTRATION: Visit the University website and click on Student – Undergraduate – Student Portal – Course Registration to complete your Course Registration online. Visit the same page to check the status of your course registration. Seek the advice of your Department Level Coordinator before completing the Form.
    2. STEP 5: PHYSICAL CLEARANCE
      1. Visit your Department and submit the documents listed below to your Departmental Administrative Officer:
        1. Acceptance Fee E-Payment Receipt.
        2. A copy of the Online School Charges Receipt.
        3. Federal University Lokoja Admission Letter.
        4. Form 01 (STUDENT BIO-DATA FORM) online printout.
        5. JAMB Result Slip (online print out) – for UTME Candidates.
        6. JAMB Admission Letter (Duplicate for Institution use only).
        7. Record from previous Institution (Statement of Result/Certificate) – for DE Candidates.
        8. O’level Result(s) (Online Print-out).
        9. Signed Letter of Undertaking (original copy).
        10. Certificate of Local Government of Origin (If Non-Nigerian, Residence Permit).
        11. Birth Certificate/Declaration of Age.
        12. Ten (10) recent Passport Photograph with red background.
        13. Medical Certificate of Fitness from the University Health Services Centre only.
        14. Completed Course Registration Forms printed from the University portal.
      2. Submit a copy of your STUDENT BIO-DATA FORM (Form 01) and the online School Charges Receipt to each of the Units listed below:
        1. Students Affairs Division.
        2. University Library.
        3. Sports Unit.
        4. University Health Services Centre.
        5. University Counseling Centre.
        6. Security Unit.

Be guided accordingly and follow the instructions diligently.

CAUTION:
  • Ensure you diligently monitor the payment of any charges (acceptance fee, school charges) to its successful completion, as there might be fraudsters who will collect money from you and present you with a fake payment receipt.
  • A copy of your payment receipt will be sent from REMITA to your registered email address on the portal as a means of confirming the successful completion of the payment process.
  • Beware of impostors who interact or send you SMS with promises of assistance in the area of admissions, registration, result processing, accommodation, etc. Federal University Lokoja maintains the best ethical practices in handling students’ matters.
  • Beware of fraudsters lurking around cafes or on & off-campus in the handling of your ATM card and bank details.
  • Report any fraudulent act or suspicious activities to the Security Unit, Federal University Lokoja.
  • If in doubt, kindly submit your inquiry to: admissions@fulokoja.edu.ng or visit the Admissions Office, Faculty of Education Building, Felele Campus, Lokoja.

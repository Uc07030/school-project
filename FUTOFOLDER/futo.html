FUTO Post UTME 2022/2023

The Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO) is yet to release the Post UTME Form for the 2022/2023 academic session. The commencement date for the sales of the form is scheduled for August 19, 2022. The eligibility criteria include a minimum JAMB score of 150 for UME candidates and selecting FUTO as the first choice for Direct Entry candidates. Applicants must be at least 16 years old and have a minimum of five O’ Level credit passes, including English language and mathematics.

The cost of the FUTO Post UTME Form is Two Thousand Naira (N2,000), with an additional recommended budget of Three Thousand Naira (N3,000) for cyber café charges. The application process involves visiting the FUTO Screening Portal, obtaining the form, and following the instructions for successful registration. The closing date for online registration is October 5, 2022, and the Post UTME exam is scheduled to commence on November 10, 2022.

It is emphasized that early commencement of registration is advised to avoid congestion, and candidates failing to submit applications online by the specified date will not be considered for admission. The management also cautions against impersonation, as it is considered a serious offense, and candidates caught may face legal consequences.

For additional information and support, applicants can contact the provided email address (ict@futo.edu.ng) or hotline (08139833300, 08060421325). The article concludes with a reminder to avoid mistakes during registration and encourages candidates to visit a reliable cyber café for assistance.

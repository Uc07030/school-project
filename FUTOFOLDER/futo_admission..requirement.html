FUTO Weekend Programmes Admission - 2020/2021 Academic Session

General University Admission Requirements:

Candidates must possess five (5) O’ Level credit passes, including Mathematics and English Language, at not more than two sittings in WASC, WASSCE, GCE, NECO, or NABTEB. Additionally, candidates must have a minimum score of 180 in the Joint Admission Matriculations Board (JAMB) Examinations.

Available Undergraduate Weekend Programmes and Entry Requirements:

  • School of Agriculture and Agricultural Technology (SAAT)

    Departments: Agricultural Economics, Agricultural Extension, Animal Science and Technology, Crop Science and Technology, Soil Science and Technology, Fisheries and Aquaculture, Forestry and Wild Life.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

  • School of Biological Sciences (SOBS)

    Departments: Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, Microbiology.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

  • School of Physical Sciences (SOPS)

    Departments: Geology, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Statistics, Science Laboratory Technology.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

  • School of Computing and Information Technology (SCIT)

    Departments: Information Technology, Computer Science.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

  • School of Health Technology

    Departments: Public Health Technology.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

  • School of Environmental Science (SOES)

    Departments: Environmental Management.

    Entry Requirements: Five O’ Level credit passes in specified subjects.

Programme Duration:

The minimum duration of the Programme is 6 years.

Application Fee:

Applicants must pay a non-refundable Application Fee of Eleven Thousand, Three Hundred Naira (11,300.00) only, in any bank via Remita.

Method of Application:

Applicants can apply by visiting the University website, generating a payment invoice, and completing the application form. Valid mobile numbers and email addresses are crucial for further communication.

Note: Candidates failing to submit applications online will not be considered for admission.

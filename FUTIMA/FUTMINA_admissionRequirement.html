General Admission Requirements for Postgraduate Studies

Postgraduate Studies in General:
  • A minimum of five credits in WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, or GCE ('O' Level), including English and Mathematics in not more than two sittings.
  • No admission granted based on awaiting results.
  • PhD candidates should hold a Master's degree or equivalent with a minimum average of 60% or a CGPA of 3.50 out of 5.00.
Master Degree Programmes:
  • A minimum of Second Class Honours (Lower Division) or a Third Class Honours with at least 3 years graduation experience in relevant fields.
  • HND with Lower Credit, OND Lower Credit, and a Postgraduate Diploma with at least Lower Credit and one year graduation experience may be considered.
  • Examinations may be required in certain cases, with an associated fee.
Postgraduate Diploma Programmes:
  • Third Class Honours Degree in relevant fields or HND with a minimum of Lower Credit, OND Lower Credit in a relevant field with one year graduation experience may be considered.
Duration of Programmes:
  • PhD: Minimum of Six Semesters (Three Academic Sessions) for Full-time Students.
  • Master Degree: Minimum of Three Semesters for Full-time Students.
  • Postgraduate Diploma: Generally, Full-time Students are expected to graduate after Three Semesters.
Specific School Requirements:
  • Departments have specific entry requirements.
  • NYSC certificate or exemption letter required.
  • Pass grades at any level do not qualify for admission to PhD/Master/PGD programmes.
  • Final decision on qualified candidates rests with the Postgraduate School Board.

School of Infrastructure, Process Engineering and Technology

The School of Infrastructure, Process Engineering and Technology encompasses the Department of Agricultural and Bioresources Engineering and the Department of Chemical Engineering. These departments offer Ph.D. (Full-time and Part-time), M.Eng (Full-time only), and PGD programs, each with detailed entry requirements based on academic performance and qualifications.

Department of Agricultural and Bioresources Engineering

The Department of Agricultural and Bioresources Engineering provides Ph.D. (Full-time and Part-time) in areas like Farm Power and Machinery, Agricultural Crop Processing and Storage, Soil and Water Engineering. M.Eng (Full-time only) is offered in the same specializations. The entry requirements vary for Ph.D., MTech, and PGD programs.

Department of Chemical Engineering

The Department of Chemical Engineering offers Ph.D. (Full-time and Part-time) and MEng (Full-time only) in Chemical Engineering. There is also a Postgraduate Diploma in Chemical Engineering (Full-time only). O’ Level Entry Requirements include a minimum of five (5) credits at G.C.E., ‘O’ level or SSCE or NECO with specific subjects. The entry requirements for Ph.D., MEng, and PGD are outlined with necessary criteria.

Postgraduate Programs in Technology Education and Related Fields

The Federal University of Technology, Minna, offers a range of postgraduate programs in various departments. The departments include:

  • Automobile Technology Education
  • Building Technology Education
  • Electrical/Electronic Technology Education
  • Metalwork Technology Education
  • Woodwork Technology Education
  • Educational Technology
  • Science Education
  • Library and Information Technology

Admission Requirements:

For all departments, 'O' Level Entry Requirements include a minimum of five credits in English Language, Mathematics, Physics, and any two other Science subjects.

Department Highlights:

Department of Educational Technology:

  • Offers Ph.D. and MTech in Educational Technology.
  • 'O' Level Entry Requirements: Five credits, including English, Mathematics, and three other Science subjects.
  • PGD Entry Requirements: Minimum of Third Class Bachelor’s Degree.

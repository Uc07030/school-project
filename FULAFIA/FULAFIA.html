FULAFIA Admission Requirements

Admission to Federal University Lafia is open to all without discrimination. Prospective students must meet Faculty and Departmental requirements along with the following general criteria:

Admissions through the UTME:

  • Candidates must have five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects at SSCE, GCE, NECO, or equivalent in not more than two sittings.
  • Attain a score of 180 and above in the UTME.
  • Must possess credit passes in English Language and Mathematics.
  • Undergo post UTME screening for final admission.

Admission through Direct Entry:

  • Candidates must have five (5) credit passes at SSCE, GCE, NECO, or equivalent, including English Language.
  • Hold at least two advanced level passes in relevant subjects, or possess an Ordinary National Diploma with credit level or Nigeria Certificate of Education (NCE) with Credit level.

For more details, refer to the FULAFIA Post UTME Form.

FULAFIA Cut off Mark 2023

The current UTME Cut off Mark for FULAFIA is 200 for Human Anatomy and Physiology, 210 for Nursing and Radiography, 220 for MLS and 230 forMBBS 170 for all others. The Federal University, Lafia, Nasarawa is inviting candidates who made FULAFIA their first choice in the recently concluded UTME for admission into their department of choice.

FULAFIA also has supplementary forms for candidates who did not meet the minimum cut off point for their department. Candidates are expected to have at least 5 Credits in their WASSCE or NECO Result in addition to scoring at least 170 in the UTME. That is the current cut off mark for all the departments in FULAFIA. Candidates who wish to apply for admission into Federal University, Lafia, Nasarawa should read about FULAFIA Post UTME and also visit FULAFIA Portal.

FULAFIA Departmental Cut Off Mark

FULAFIA Departmental cut off mark is 170. Subsequent changes to this cut off mark will be updated on The Federal University, Lafia, Nasarawa website. It has been stated that only candidates who put FULAFIA as their first choice are eligible to apply.

FULAFIA Departmental Cut Off Mark

  1. Human Anatomy and Physiology – 200 and above
  2. Nursing and Radiography – 210
  3. Medical Laboratory Science – 220
  4. Medicine and Surgery (MBBS) – 230
  5. Other FULAFIA Courses – 170

Note: The cut off mark for each varies depending on the performance of candidates who applied to study in the school for that year.

FULAFIA Post-UTME Eligibility:

  • Candidates who score exactly or above the required cut off mark.
  • Candidates who chose Federal University Lafia as their preferred choice of institution during the UTME Registration.
  • Candidates are also required to obtain the required O’level credits for their programmes as specified in JAMB Brochure.
  • Candidate must be at least 16 years of age
  • Interested Candidates who did not choose Federal University Lafia during the UTME Registration but wish to study in the University should change to the FULAFIA via JAMB website.

N/B: It is very easy to obtain admission if one has met the required cut off mark for admission into a particular course

ABU Zaria Postgraduate School Fees 2022/2023 This is to inform the public that the management at the Ahmadu Bello University ABU Zaria has released the school fees for the postgraduate Students for the 2022/2023 Academic Session.

The School of Postgraduate Studies at the Ahmadu Bello University accommodates the PGD, Masters’, Doctoral, and even Post-Doctoral programmes. Understandably, the fees are far more expensive than those of undergraduate courses. Note that to apply for any of these programmes, you have to purchase the application form which costs N10, 500.

Acceptance Fees: ABU requested a sum of N50,000 for both Sciences and School of Management Sciences. This is the same amount paid by Post-Graduate Diploma Students. Registration Fee: N25, 000 for PGD and Masters, both Sciences and Management Sciences.

School Fees: N150, 000 for Postgraduate Diploma, while Masters students pay between N200,000 and N250,000 depending on the course in ABU. Health, Computer, Examination, and Sports Fees: All of these fees amount to about N120, 000. Developmental Levy: N100, 0000 to be paid by every postgraduate student.

Total Fees Payable at the end of Programme: While a Masters Programme in the Sciences will cost about N510, 000 intuition, Management Sciences will cost N455,000, while a PGD student will be paying N405,000 as at the end of the final semester. These fees do not include the cost of lodging, food, or any other personal goods you may require. Typically, the school provides hostel accommodation to students who wish to reside on campus, and ABU students pay the lowest hostel room fees in Nigeria.

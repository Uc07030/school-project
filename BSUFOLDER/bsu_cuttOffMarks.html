Benue State University (BSU) - JAMB Cut-Off Marks for 2022/2023

The article provides information about the Benue State University (BSU) and its JAMB cut-off marks for the 2022/2023 academic session. It emphasizes the importance of these cut-off marks for candidates who selected BSU as their preferred choice during UTME registration.

The Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB) set a general cut-off mark of 160 for universities in Nigeria, and BSU adjusted its minimum cut-off mark accordingly. The article states that candidates scoring 180 and above are eligible for the BSU post-UTME screening exercise.

The piece addresses frequently asked questions (FAQs) related to BSU cut-off marks, acceptance of second choice candidates, changing courses, and subject combinations for specific courses. It also highlights the importance of scoring above the cut-off mark for increased chances of admission.

The article concludes by providing the specific JAMB cut-off marks for various departments and faculties at BSU for the 2022/2023 academic session. It advises candidates to meet the minimum requirements, including O'level results and other documents, for eligibility in the internal screening process.

Additionally, it mentions that scoring above the minimum cut-off mark does not guarantee automatic admission, as successful completion of the internal screening exercise is necessary for admission consideration.

BSU Undergraduate Admission Requirements:

UTME Entry:

  • Age requirement: Minimum of 16 years.
  • O'level Result: At least 6 "Cs" grades, including English Language, Mathematics, and three other relevant subjects.
  • JAMB UTME Score: Minimum of 180.
  • BSU Post UTME: Mandatory with a satisfactory performance.
  • One sitting result is accepted.

Direct Entry:

  • O'level Result: At least five (5) credits, including English Language and Mathematics, in a single sitting.
  • Age requirement: Minimum of 16 years.
  • Obtain JAMB Direct Entry form.
  • Acceptable certificates: OND, ND, NCE, IJMB, Cambridge A level, BSc, HND, ICAN.

In summary, BSU offers a broad range of undergraduate programs, and admission can be secured through either UTME or Direct Entry, each with specific criteria. It emphasizes academic excellence, age criteria, correct subject combinations, and meeting stipulated cutoff marks.

BSUM Freshers’ Clearance & Registration Procedure – 2016/2017

The management of Benue State University, Makurdi (BSUM) has announced the clearance and registration procedure for all newly admitted students for the 2016/2017 Academic Session.

  1. Check for your admission status on BSU Admission List and print details on payment of N400 at the designated banks;
  2. Proceed to pay the acceptance fee of Twenty Two thousand naira (N22,000) non-refundable to Benue State University, Makurdi at the designated banks and print your admission letter;
  3. Candidates who had earlier paid an acceptance fee of Five thousand, Five hundred naira (N5,500) only should pay an additional sum of Sixteen Thousand, Five Hundred naira (N16,500) to make up the Twenty Two thousand naira (N22,000) only to enable them to print the admission letter.

The documents are as follows:

  • Original academic qualifications
  • Original JAMB Result slip with embossed passport photograph
  • Original Direct Entry Online e-registration printout with embossed passport photograph
  • Birth certificate / declaration of age
  • Certificate of State Origin

Candidates who are yet to upload their credentials and other documents on the portal are advised to do so to enable us to screen them Online.

Complete the screening form Online, print and sign. Your screening status will be communicated to you in the remarks column on the form;

Proceed to Academic Office for documentation;

Completion of Registration and Matriculation Details

After successfully completing your registration, you are now prepared for matriculation, scheduled for Thursday, 19th May 2016. Failure to register will result in non-matriculation and subsequent disqualification of your admission.

Please be attentive to upcoming orientation and matriculation programs.

Important Reminders:

  • Registration by proxy is PROHIBITED.
  • Change of name(s) is PROHIBITED.

Checklist of Requirements for Screening and Registration:

  1. Processing fee receipt of payment of twenty-two thousand naira (N22,000)
  2. BSU Admission Letter
  3. Post-UTME and Direct Entry screening test result
  4. Original JAMB admission letter (Check JAMB website)
  5. Original UTME result slip with embossed passport photograph
  6. Original Direct Entry online e-registration printout with embossed passport photograph
  7. Original of academic qualifications
  8. Birth certificate / declaration of age
  9. Certificate of State of Origin
  10. Writing materials
  11. Four (4) passport photographs

note!Candidates who are unsuccessful at the screening process may wish to report at the Academic Office with their screening forms and other credentials for further directives.
